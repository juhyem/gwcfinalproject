Organizing a protest tips:

- Set a goal, make sure you know what message you are trying to get across
- Choose a practical location, somewhere symbolic and convenient
- Pick a time that you will have the greatest audience to make a bigger impact
- Publicize your protest! Make sure you know people who are coming you can use this site to help you spd the word.


Make sure you are prepared for a protest. Here are some tips:

- Know your rights
- Make sure you dress comfortably

-It's important to protect your personal belongings, wear a zippered backpack and be weary of others in large crowds
-Wear padding and shatter proof eyewear in case there is any police batons and pepper spray.

- Pack a First-Aid kit in case you take a small tumble or twist your ankle.
- Be smart, know when it is time to leave.
